Year 1 - 6 Program Details

Year 1 - 6

Welcome to Sathitpathum Demonstration School, an institution dedicated to providing quality education for students in Year 1 to 6. Our program is designed to foster academic excellence, personal growth, and holistic development among our students. With a strong emphasis on student-centered learning and a supportive environment, we strive to prepare our students for future success.

At Sathitpathum Demonstration School, we follow a comprehensive curriculum that aligns with national educational standards. Our curriculum encompasses a wide range of subjects, including language arts, mathematics, science, social studies, physical education, arts, and technology. The curriculum is designed to be engaging, relevant, and age-appropriate, ensuring that students acquire essential knowledge and skills at each grade level.
