Kinder 3 Program Details

Kinder 3

The Kinder 3 program at Sathitpathum Demonstration School is a comprehensive educational curriculum designed to provide a nurturing and stimulating environment for children aged 5 to 6 years. Our program focuses on holistic development, encompassing cognitive, social, emotional, and physical aspects to ensure a well-rounded educational experience for each child.
